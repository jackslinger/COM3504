A Social Media Search Bot Developed for COM3504

Developed by Jack Slinger and Max Rilley

Welcome to our social media search bot. We utilise Twitter, Foursquare, Wikipedia and Google Maps to provide a service that can keep track of particular users and locations by running both searches through old data, as well as providing streaming functionality that captures live tweets to update in real time.

In order to better highlight the technology used behind the scenes, comments will be given at the top of each page explaining how that part of the system functions. This should give a better understanding of how different web technologies can be used to perform complex tasks such as keeping track of indivduals.

This site is made for the COM3504 module of the Sheffield University Computer Science course, this site is not intended for use monitoring people and great care has been taken to follow the terms and conditions of all the used services.
